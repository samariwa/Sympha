About Shympha

Based in Korompoi, Kitengela, Shympha was established three and a half years ago with only one staff and now has 5 staff members. It has since grown into a medium-sized poultry products production and distribution company.

During this time, the company has positioned itself as an organic chicken and eggs supplier for restaurants and low to middle income households in Nairobi.

With a wide range of chicken products, affordable prices, time bound supplies and excellent customer support, we have secured a good name.

Mission

To be the leading door-to-door poultry products supplier supplying within 3 hours of order placement.

Vision

To make poultry products affordable to many.
