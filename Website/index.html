Why Shympha?

Shympha enterprises scores high on price, quality and freshness.
But it doesn’t stop there.

Transparency

The business uplifts the standards of living of household and restuarant value chain members by providing means through which food production is made efficient thus saving costs, effort,and valuable time.

Innovation

Through the use of sustainable renewable energy for the preparation, production and storage of food, the business promotes sustainable production of food.

Sustainability

The business strives for accountability and transparency in its governance enabled through automation and the use of the latest ICT innovations & advancements.
